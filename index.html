Why Norway 🇳🇴

🏛️

Society that invests in its people

I could earn high salaries in places like Dubai. But what draws me is a society that genuinely reinvests in its citizens — where high taxes translate into real, visible value: healthcare, education, and trust. That exchange matters to me deeply.

📊

Human Development Index

Norway consistently ranks at the top of the HDI — not by accident, but by design. Strong social welfare, exceptional education, and workforce productivity that signals something most countries are still figuring out.

🛢️

The Sovereign Wealth Fund — a masterclass in long-term thinking

The way Norway manages its oil fund is something I genuinely admire. Rather than spending windfall wealth, Norway built one of the largest sovereign wealth funds in the world and invested it in the future. As a designer who thinks in systems and long-term outcomes, this kind of institutional foresight resonates with how I work.

⚖️

Work-life balance that's real

Scandinavian work culture is built around trust, flat hierarchies, and depth over busyness. I'm looking for a culture where thoughtful work is valued — and Norway leads the world in exactly that.

🤝

Flat hierarchies, meaningful work

Coming from years of enterprise design across JP Morgan Chase, Teradata, and Oracle — I've collaborated across teams and cultures. Norway's collaborative, low-ego work culture is the environment I want to grow in.
